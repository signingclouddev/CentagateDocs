# 🔓Unbind Device from User

To unbind device from account, follow below steps.

### Step 1️⃣: 
From the navigation bar, hover your mouse over to **“Self Service → Authentication Methods”**.

### Step 2️⃣: 
On the **“Authentication Method”** page, select **“Device”**.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/device4.png" 
    width="400" 
    height="350" 
    alt="Device (4)"
  />
</p>


### Step 3️⃣: 
On the **“Manage Device”** page, select **“Unregister”** on the device that you wish to remove/unregister from current account.
