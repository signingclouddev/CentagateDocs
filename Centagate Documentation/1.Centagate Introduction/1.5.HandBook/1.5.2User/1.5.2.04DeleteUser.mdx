# 🗑️Delete User

As an administrator, you may encounter situations where certain users no longer exist within your company. In such cases, it becomes necessary to remove their details from the list. To eliminate unwanted or unused user profiles, follow the steps outlined below.

### Step 1️⃣: 
On the navigation bar, click **Users → User List**.

### Step 2️⃣: 
In the user list, locate the **'Action' button**, and within its options, you will find the **'Update' button**. Click on it to proceed.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/delete_1.jpg"

    alt="Delete User (1)"
  />
</p>

### Step 3️⃣: 
On the bottom of the Update User’s page, you will find a **Delete’ button**. Click on it.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/delete_2.jpg" 
    width="600" 
    height="250" 
    alt="Delete User (2)"
  />
</p>

### Step 4️⃣: 
Confirm your deletion action by clicking the **'Yes' button**.
