# 👋🏻 First-Time Login

### 🌟 Welcome to CENTAGATE! Your Journey Begins Here as a First-Time User! 🚀 

Let's tailor your account to perfection! Follow these steps for a seamless setup experience.

### Step 2️⃣: 
The link will direct you to a new page, displayed as follows:
&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/ft_2.png" 
    width="400" 
    height="450" 
    alt="First time login (2)"
  />
</p>


In this page, you have to provide:

- ***New Password*** - Set a fresh password for your account.
- ***Confirm New Password*** - Ensure the accuracy of the password by confirming it.


Once your new password is set, click the **'Next'** button to proceed. 


>❗ ***Before saving your password, ensure it meets the specified criteria***.


### Step 3️⃣: 
On the next page, you'll need to provide:

- ***Security Phrase*** - Your confidential passphrase.
- ***Security Image*** - A secret image for authentication.
- ***Question*** - The query that will be asked during Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
- ***Answer*** - Your responses to the question provided.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/ft_3.png" 
    width="350" 
    height="550" 
    alt="First time login (3)"
  />
</p>

Once your security module is set, click the 'Next' button to proceed.

>ℹ️ ***Please note that you can only select from the provided security images for your account; uploading your own image is not an option.***


### Step 4️⃣: 
🎉 **Congratulations!** You are one step closer to completing the setup of your account!

After finishing all the steps above, you will be redirected to the page below:

    width="400" 
    height="250" 
    alt="First time login (3)"
  />
</p>

Please click the **'Continue' button** to proceed to the login page.



## 🌟🚀 First-Time Login Guide

Wondering how to log in to CENTAGATE after successfully creating and setting up your account? For a smooth start, follow the steps below:

After successfully setup your account and click ‘Continue’ button, you will be redirected to CENTAGATE CLOUD login page.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/start_2.png" 
    width="600" 
    height="350" 
    alt="Getting started (2)"
  />
</p>

Insert your registered **username and click ‘Log In’ button**. Provide your password, the same password you set during account setup.
