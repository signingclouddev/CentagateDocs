# 🌟Getting Started

### Welcome to CENTAGATE CLOUD! Account Creation Journey 
#### 🥳We're thrilled to have you on board! 


🌟🚀 **Let's Get You Started on CENTAGATE!** Before diving into CENTAGATE adventures, you'll need a friendly account in CENTAGATE CLOUD. Let this guide walk you through the simple steps to create your account and unlock a world of possibilities! 🌈🚀


### Step 1️⃣: 
Have access to CENTAGATE using below link.

🔗https://console.centagatecloud.com/

### Step 3️⃣: 
Upon clicking, you'll be seamlessly redirected to a new page to kickstart your account creation process.

In this page, you have to provide:

- ***Username*** - Your username that will be use as your identity.

- ***User email*** - Your email address that accessible to receive emails.

- ***Company name*** - Your company name.

- ***Company domain*** - Your company domain.

>ℹ️ ***Before proceed with creating your account, it is advisable to carefully read and review the Terms and Conditions***.

>❗ ***Ensure a smooth account creation process by filling in all the required fields***.

### Step 4️⃣: 
To proceed, click the 'Register' button located at the bottom of the page.
