# 📎Template Setup

In Centagate, you are allowed to create your own template for SMS and Email function. Under this function, you are able to see several options of template that you may need to create. 

To create the template, you may need to set by the template type. In Centagate, we provide you 2 types of templates which is:  
1. Email Template
2. SMS Template.

### 📬Email Template

For email template, you may have few options to set, which is:  
1. Device Provision Code
2. User activation
3. Reset Password

To start set your own template, follow below steps.


### Step 1️⃣: 
From the navigation bar, go to **“Settings → Email Template or SMS Template”**.

### Step 2️⃣: 
#### Email Template

On the template page, if you choose Email Template, you may see as below interface.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.5 Hanbook/template3.png" 
    width="400" 
    height="500" 
    alt="Template (3)"
  />
</p>

In this template, you may need to provide as below:  
- ***Template for*** - There will be selection on what kind of template you want to set.
- ***Subject*** - The subject of the email that will be send to user.
- ***Message*** - The body or input of the email.

> 🌟 To make sure the message in the template display correctly, you may read or refer to the “Information” section on the bottom of each template, the information will guide you on which code to display your message correctly.

#### SMS Template

If you choose SMS template, you may have similar view as below interface.

&#x20;

In this template, you may need to provide as below:  
- ***Template for*** - There will be selection on what kind of template you want to set.
- ***Message*** - The body or input of the email.

>❗ Make sure to fill all the required details that has been marked with **asterisk (*) symbol**. Asterisk symbol under the **‘Information Details’** need to be included in the message body.


### Step 3️⃣: 
After insert all the fields required, you may click the **“Save”** button to save your template.
