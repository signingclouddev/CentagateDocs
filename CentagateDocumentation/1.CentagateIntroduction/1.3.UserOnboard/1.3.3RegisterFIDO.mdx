# 🔏 Register FIDO

To manage or register FIDO, login to CENTAGATE Cloud then go to ***“Self Service → Authentication Methods.”***

You will see a summary of your available authentication methods and token or certificates that are registered with you.

### 1. Passkey
Follow the instruction by scan the QR code with your device camera. Once you scan the QR code, your device will establish the connection to your computer.

<br />

The device will ask permission to allow to connect with QR, follow by create passkey.

### 3. Enable Passwordless Authentication

You must enable the passwordless authentication option in order to experience the passwordless authentication. Please back to the ***“Self-Service → Authentication Methods”***, you will notice that additional option appears on the screen as below. Please click the switch button to turn on the passwordless authentication.

&#x20;    
<p align="center">
  <img 
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/signingclouddev/CentagateDocs/main/image/1.3 User Onboard/1.3.3 Register FIDO/registerfido(9).png" 
    width="600" 
    height="400" 
    alt="Register FIDO (9)"
  />
</p>

<br />

### 4. Login Experience

Now you are ready to passwordless login, you may logout and re-login again. You will notice that CENTAGATE Cloud will no longer asking password for the login. Instead, it will show the FIDO login option. 
Click the FIDO icon, it may prompt to insert security key. Please click ***“Cancel”*** if you are not using the physical hardware token.

:::tip
💡 You may not experience this if login using phone mobile browser.
:::
